Beta Testing Notice

Please read the following information before using SNAPBRIFY. This document outlines what the platform does, how your data is handled, and the current limitations of the beta environment.

01. What is SNAPBRIFY?

SNAPBRIFY is a browser-based PBR (Physically Based Rendering) texture generation tool. Upload any photograph and the engine automatically derives a complete set of material maps — Base Color, Roughness, Ambient Occlusion, Height, Metallic, and Normal — ready to use in any 3D application, game engine, or design pipeline. No software installation is required; everything runs in your browser.

02. Beta Status

The platform is currently in open beta. You may encounter unexpected behaviour, performance inconsistencies, or temporary downtime as we continue development. We appreciate your patience and welcome any feedback sent through the Contact page.

03. Privacy & Data Collection

We are committed to keeping this tool lightweight and non-intrusive. During the beta period:

  • shield
    No user data is collected. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data. Your email address is used only to deliver your download link and is not stored beyond that session.
  • cloud_off
    Uploaded images are temporary. All uploaded files and generated texture sets are automatically deleted from our servers 48 hours after the project is created. Do not rely on SNAPBRIFY as a permanent storage solution.

04. Current Limits & Fair Use

To ensure fair access for all users during the beta period, the following limits are in effect:

photo_size_select_large
Max Image Size 2 MB per upload

Images larger than 2 MB will be automatically compressed before processing.

schedule
Project Retention 48 hours

All projects and generated files are permanently deleted 48 hours after creation.

person
Per-Account Daily Quota 3 uploads per account

Each account may submit up to 3 texture generation jobs per day.

groups
Global Daily Capacity 200 accounts per day

A maximum of 200 unique accounts may upload per day across the entire platform. Once the cap is reached, uploads are paused until the daily reset.

05. Daily Reset

alarm

All quotas reset daily at 23:59 Taiwan Standard Time (UTC+8).

This includes both the per-account upload count and the platform-wide 200-account capacity. If uploads are unavailable, please check back after the daily reset.

06. Disclaimer

SNAPBRIFY is provided as-is during the beta period. We do not guarantee continuous availability, accuracy of generated materials, or preservation of uploaded files beyond the stated 48-hour window. By using the platform, you acknowledge these limitations and agree not to use it for storing irreplaceable assets.
