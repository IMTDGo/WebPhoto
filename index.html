What is Snapbrify

From a Photograph
to a Production‑Ready Material

SNAPBRIFY turns any surface you can photograph — wood, stone, fabric, leather, concrete — into a complete, seamless PBR texture set, ready for game engines and 3D software.

Everything happens directly in your browser. Capture with your phone, refine the crop, and the engine computes every channel map on your device — then delivers the finished set as a ZIP or straight to your inbox.
